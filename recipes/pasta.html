pasta is a delicious dish made with tomatoes, onion, garlic and dried hurbs. making italian style pasta is easy and quick for breakfast. you can use fresh tomatoes, canned tomatoes or tomato paste to make souce. This is one dish which i make quite often. you can make this pasta under 30 minutes which makes it family faviourate meal on busy days.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Dried Hurbs

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with 2 liters of water. Add 1 tablespoon of salt to the water (this helps season the pasta as it cooks). Bring the water to a rolling boil over high heat.
  2. Add 200g of pasta to the boiling water. Stir immediately to prevent the pasta from sticking together. Cook according to the package instructions (usually 8-10 minutes) until the pasta is al dente (firm to the bite). Drain the pasta using a colander, but save a cup of the pasta water for later.
  3. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, but not browned. Add the diced tomatoes or tomato sauce to the skillet. Stir in 1 teaspoon of dried oregano or basil, and ¼ teaspoon of black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked pasta into the skillet with the sauce. Toss the pasta in the sauce until fully coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up. Let it cook together for 1-2 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  5. Divide the pasta into bowls. Top with grated Parmesan cheese (optional) and garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
